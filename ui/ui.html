    Pro tip

    Having accurate measurements helps us provide better alteration services. Visit our kiosk for professional measurements!

    How to Measure

    For best results, wear fitted clothing or undergarments. Keep the measuring tape snug but not tight.

    1

    Bust

    Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

    2

    Waist

    Measure at your natural waistline - the narrowest part of your torso, usually above the belly button.

    3

    Hips

    Measure around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks, keeping tape parallel to floor.

    4

    Shoulder

    Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the other, across your upper back.

    Need help? Visit our kiosk for professional measurements - it's complimentary with any service!
